About

I'm a Computer Science graduate from Gunadarma University with extensive experience in technical support and software testing. I started my career at PT Bank Central Asia Tbk. and PT BCA Finance, where I gained valuable insights into technical support and quality assurance. Currently, I am working as a QA Automation Engineer at PT Bank Syariah Indonesia Tbk. In this role, I focus on automating processes to enhance efficiency and quality. My responsibilities include designing, developing, and executing automated test scripts using the Selenium Pytest framework with Python or UFT, and implementing the Page Object Model in Selenium to organize and structure the test scripts. I also generate comprehensive PDF reports using Python and use Allure Report to ensure testing outcomes are clearly communicated.
